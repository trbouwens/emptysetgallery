BÜS

Various Artists
Curated by Joshua O'Grady

The question that has persisted throughout human history has been: how do we get from here to there? Modern science gave us a definitive answer: bus. This gallery curated by contributor Joshua O'Grady examines the beauty of the most elegant mode of transportation we have in our society.Truly, is there any better way to get around than the bus? I bet if you ask anyone in the universe about their experience with busses, they can sit down with you for hours and happily reminice about happy memories of busses in their lives. I personally remember a time in eigth grade when we took a bus to Chicago for band and we spent the entire drive there and back on the bus playing Mario Kart DS and betting real money on the outcomes of the races. I was the booky and managed to go the whole trip without any teachers or chaperones finding out about our borderline illegal gambling. Another time, I was in New York City riding the bus to the Port Authority and had a fellow passanger scream at me about drugs for about seven minutes before he collapsed and we had to call the paramedics. If I weren't on the bus then I would never have had that memorable experience.
