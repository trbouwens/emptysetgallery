Twisted Corporations

Our contributor Josh O'Grady presents his collection of corporate mascots that are slightly twisted with strange versions of their slogans. This examines our relationship with our corporate overlords and makes us consider what they force us to do. Very insightful and sophisticated.

EXPLORE →

Flags

A nation's identity is often demonstrated by a proud flag waving above it. In this gallery, our contributor Nelson Pires explores a simplistic, modern approach to the identities of multiple nations in their flags.

EXPLORE →

Abstract Art

Every once in a while a piece of art comes along that makes you ask: what is art? Art can make us feel happy, sad, tired, or any other emotion the artist wants you to feel. These images created by our contributors certainly make us feel a specific emotion: confusion.

EXPLORE →

Bus

The question that has persisted throughout human history has been: how do we get from here to there? Modern science gave us a definitive answer: bus. This gallery curated by contributor Joshua O'Grady examines the beauty of the most elegant mode of transportation we have in our society.

EXPLORE →

Paint Gallery

This gallery is comprised of an exquisite selection of the highest-quality paints discovered and maintained by our acquaintances at The Home Depot. From the majestic Anime to the burning introspection of 100 Miles Per Hour, this collection contains shades to excite and inspire all who gaze upon it.
